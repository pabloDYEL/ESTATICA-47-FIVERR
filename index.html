Frequently Asked Questions

What is Voky?

Voky is an evolving conversational voice AI that is in beta and currently capable of sending or receiving millions of phone calls p/ day with voice agents that sound like a human.

How can I create my first voice agent?

After signing into Voky (it’s FREE to start!) you can press on Add Agent and follow the steps inside.It’s very quick!

What type of tasks can Voky perform?

Please keep in mind that Voky is in beta! Voky can currently call your contacts and initiate campaigns to qualify your prospects and then send that information with a transcription and summary of the call to your CRM.As we progress and release API docs, you will be able to unlock many other scenarios like setting Voky up directly with a calendar for appointment reminders, customer service use cases, and more.

Can I integrate Voky with other tools or systems?

Yes! Connect your webhook and leads will enter your system once qualified.

What are the pricing plans and billing options?

You can start using Voky now for free! There are multiple pricing options available within the app.

Is there customer support or training available?

We have several training documents available and more are being released on a daily basis.Tier 3 pricing does include two onboarding sessions to diagnose and help you quick- start your campaigns.

Is there a free trial or demo available?

Free! Free, free! Sign into Voky with Talk to Voky and begin from there... Did we mention, free?

Can I customize the voices of my agents?

Yes. As of now Voky supports up to a dozen different voice styles. We are releasing more on a daily basis.In the future, deep- fake options will be available as well.

What languages does Voky support?

Voky is currently in beta and operating with English. But, Voky currently supports over 20 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Portuguese, Hindi, Arabic, etc and these will be included into the platform Q1

How many calls can be executed per day?

Voky is capable of sending or receiving millions of calls per day.

What use cases is Voky best suited for?

Anyone that utilizes marketing to schedule appointments can benefit from Voky. This includes Real Estate Wholesalers, Real Estate Agents, Real Estate Brokers, and Real Estate Investors.

Do I have to build my own Agent? Are there any pre-built options?

We do offer basic pre-built options that you can begin building off of. You are able to diagnose and set up your agent within our app Agent Testing Suite.
